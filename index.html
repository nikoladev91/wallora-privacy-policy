Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Wallora ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information.

Information Collection

Wallora does not require users to create an account. The App does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses or phone numbers.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information, including advertising identifiers, device identifiers, IP address and diagnostic information, to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements, measure advertising performance and prevent fraud.

For more information please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Favorites

Favorite wallpapers are stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

Wallpaper Downloads

When downloading wallpapers, images are saved directly to your device's gallery. The developer does not receive any information about downloaded wallpapers.

Data Security

The App uses secure encrypted connections (HTTPS) whenever network communication occurs.

Children's Privacy

Wallora does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-party Services

The App uses Google AdMob. Their Privacy Policy is available at:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be published on this page.
